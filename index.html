About Me

I am currently a Data & Applied Scientist in the Voice Assistant Team, Flipkart (Walmart Group). Here, I work closely with Dr Sanjeev Kumar and focus on building efficient and accurate deep neural models for speech and language processing in Indic languages. Outside my regular working hours, I also collaborate on various research projects with Prof Rajiv Ratn Shah at MIDAS Lab, IIIT Delhi, where I am currently working on improving the robustness of deep neural models for speech recognition. I graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University) Varanasi in 2018 with a B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering. During my undergraduate studies, I interned at MICS Lab, CentraleSupélec, France and worked with Prof Céline Hudelot on multimodal document image segmentation using deep learning. Under the supervision of Prof Kaushal Kumar Shukla, I completed my bachelor’s project on face anti-spoofing.

I also maintained an interest in programming (Codeforces, HackerRank) and software development as an undergrad. I qualified for ACM ICPC India Regionals 2016 and contributed to MS Excel while interning at Microsoft.

I love writing (Medium, Quora) technical blogs. When away from computers, I love running marathons and strumming my Strat.
