# About
I'm a Machine Learning (ML) Researcher/Engineer at Verneek AI, focusing on developing personalized e-commerce search. Previously, I have led applied research in speech recognition and synthesis at Walmart India, playing a key role in developing a <a href="https://stories.flipkart.com/use-voice-assistant-to-buy-groceries-flipkart/">multilingual voice assistant</a>. I hold a Master's in Computer Science (CS) from Georgia Tech.  My CS academic journey began at IIT Varanasi, where I graduated with top honors in my class. Please find more details in my CV.
